Teriyaki Chicken

Description

A dish of cooked rice stir-fried with chicken, vegetables, eggs, and seasonings.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Cornstarch
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Chicken meat
  • Soy sauce
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Combine sugar, soy sauce, cider vinegar, cornstarch, cold water, garlic, ginger, and pepper in a small saucepan over low heat.
  4. Simmer, stirring frequently, until teriyaki sauce thickens and bubbles, 3 to 5 minutes.
  5. Remove from the heat.
  6. Place chicken thighs in the prepared baking dish.
  7. Brush both sides of each thigh with the sauce.
  8. Reserve any extra sauce for basting.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  10. Flip chicken and brush with sauce.
  11. Continue to bake, basting with remaining sauce every 10 minutes, until no longer pink and juices run clear, 20 to 30 more minutes.
