Chicken Fried Rice

Description

A dish of cooked rice stir-fried with chicken, vegetables, eggs, and seasonings.

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • Water
  • Butter
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onion
  • White rice
  • Chicken meat
  • Soy sauce
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Beat egg and water together in small bowl.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Melt butter in a large skillet or wok over medium-low heat.
  4. Add egg and cook without stirring for 1 to 2 minutes.
  5. Remove egg from skillet and cut into shreds then set aside.
  6. Heat oil in the same skillet.
  7. Add onion then cook and stir until soft for about 3 minutes.
  8. Stir in rice, chicken, soy sauce, and pepper.
  9. Cook and stir for about 5 minutes.
  10. Stir in shredded egg.
